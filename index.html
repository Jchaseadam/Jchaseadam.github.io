Our Mission

Our mission is to help musicians and fans alike. The problem we have run into as music fans ourselves is that many of our favorite musicians fail to recognize Athens as a profitable place to make a tour stop. We would like to bridge the gap between venues and musicians by seeking out talent for venues and booking them at a venue in Athens.

The Team

Charlie Haas and Chase Adam come from completely different musical backgrounds and have varying musical tastes. The combination of the two allows a larger amount of people to see the musical acts they love in the place they call home.
